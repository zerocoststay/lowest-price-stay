Explore the Mystical North East

Beyond the clouds lies a land of untouched beauty and vibrant heritage.

North East India, the "Land of the Seven Sisters and One Brother," is a paradise for nature lovers and seekers of authentic culture. It is a region where the sun greets India first, lighting up emerald tea gardens, ancient monasteries, and the purest river waters in Asia.

Pure Biodiversity: Witness the majestic One-Horned Rhino in Assam and the mysterious floating islands of Manipur.
Vibrant Heritage: Home to over 200 diverse tribes, each celebrating life through unique folk music, hand-woven crafts, and colourful festivals.
Ethereal Peaks: Trek through the misty living root bridges of Meghalaya or find peace in the snow-capped mountains of Arunachal Pradesh.
Untouched Flavors: Embark on a culinary journey with organic, exotic ingredients and traditional fermented delicacies unique to the region.
