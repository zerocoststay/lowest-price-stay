Minstay – India’s Lowest Cost Stay Platform

Minstay is India’s lowest cost stay discovery platform designed to help students, working professionals, and travelers find affordable short-term stays and long-term PG accommodations with ease. The platform focuses on minimum cost living, making it easier to settle in a new city without paying unnecessary premium prices.

Whether you are staying for a few days or planning long-term living, Minstay helps you discover budget-friendly stays across India. Unlike traditional hotel or rental platforms, Minstay prioritizes affordability, simplicity, and real user needs over luxury listings.

Minstay is especially useful for students searching for PG accommodations near colleges, interns relocating temporarily, and professionals moving to a new city for work. The platform reduces the hassle of dealing with brokers and inflated rents by making affordable options easier to find.

With Minstay, users can explore short-term stays for temporary needs and also connect with long-term PG options through a single platform. As Minstay expands across India, its mission remains clear — to make affordable stays accessible to everyone.
