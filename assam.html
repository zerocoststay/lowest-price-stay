Frequently Asked Questions About Hometays in Guwahati

What is the starting price of a homestay in Guwahati?

A homestay in Guwahati can start from just ₹499 per night, depending on the location, amenities, and season. Budget-friendly options are available for solo travellers, couples, families, and business visitors.

Which is the best homestay in Guwahati for families?

The best homestay in Guwahati for families is one that offers spacious rooms, a safe environment, parking, Wi-Fi, and easy access to popular attractions like Kamakhya Temple, GS Road, and Umananda Island.

Can I book a homestay in Guwahati for one night?

Yes, most homestays in Guwahati accept one-night bookings. Whether you're visiting for tourism, business, or a transit stay, you can find flexible accommodation for short stays.

Are there budget homestays in Guwahati near the railway station?

Yes, you can find a budget homestay in Guwahati near Guwahati Railway Station and Paltan Bazaar, making it convenient for travellers arriving by train.

Can I find a homestay in Guwahati near the airport?

Yes, several homestays in Guwahati are located near Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport, making them ideal for early morning or late-night flights.

Which areas are best for booking a homestay in Guwahati?

The most popular areas for a homestay in Guwahati include Ganeshguri, GS Road, Beltola, Six Mile, Khanapara, Paltan Bazaar, and Pan Bazaar because of their connectivity and nearby facilities.

Is a homestay in Guwahati better than a hotel?

A homestay in Guwahati is often more affordable than a hotel while offering a comfortable, home-like environment, personalized hospitality, and excellent value for money.

Can couples book a homestay in Guwahati?

Yes, many homestays in Guwahati welcome couples and provide comfortable, private rooms with modern amenities. Guests should carry valid government-issued ID during check-in.

Is breakfast included with a homestay in Guwahati?

Some homestays in Guwahati include complimentary breakfast, while others offer it as an optional service. The available facilities may vary from one property to another.

How early should I book a homestay in Guwahati?

It is recommended to book a homestay in Guwahati at least one to two weeks in advance during the tourist season, especially between October and April, to get better availability and prices.

Are homestays in Guwahati suitable for business travellers?

Yes, many homestays in Guwahati offer high-speed Wi-Fi, peaceful surroundings, and easy access to commercial areas, making them a great choice for business travellers.

What amenities can I expect in a homestay in Guwahati?

Most homestays in Guwahati provide clean rooms, attached bathrooms, Wi-Fi, air conditioning, parking, hot water, and convenient access to local transport and restaurants.
