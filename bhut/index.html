BHUT – An Ethnic Pickle with Fire

BHUT is a handcrafted pickle made from the legendary Bhut Jolokia, rooted in the ethnic food traditions of Northeast India. Bold, intense, and authentic, it delivers real heat without compromising on flavour or heritage.


Made in small batches using traditional spices and natural oil, BHUT is slow-processed to preserve the chilli’s raw power, aroma, and texture — nothing artificial, nothing diluted.

Bhut Jolokia – The World’s Hottest Ghost Chilli

Bhut Jolokia, or Ghost Chilli, originates from Northeast India and is known for crossing 1,000,000 Scoville Heat Units. Its heat builds slowly and lasts longer than most chillies, making it truly legendary.


Grown in warm, humid regions, Bhut Jolokia is prized worldwide for its intensity and cultural significance, setting it apart from ordinary peppers.
