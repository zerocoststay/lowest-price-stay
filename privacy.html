1. Introduction

Welcome to Minstay ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and mobile application (the "Platform"). We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

To provide you with our booking services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Identity Verification: ID proof details required for property verification.
  • Booking Details: Information regarding your stay duration and preferences.

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

We respect your privacy. We share necessary information (such as your Name and Booking details) only with the specific PG or Homestay partner where you have made a booking to ensure fulfillment. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, marketing agencies, or external organizations.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, including HTTPS encryption, to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

Our platform may use cookies to enhance user experience, remember your login session, and analyze usage patterns to improve our services.

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to delete your account or data from our servers, please contact us at the email address provided below, and we will process your request within 7 business days.
